Obscure Settings

Enter the URL patterns (one per line) for the pages you want to hide. The page will show a fake "site can't be reached" message. Type your 4-digit PIN anywhere and press Enter to unlock.

Pattern Examples (Use one per line above):
  • https://www.example.com/private/page.html
  • https://www.example.com/private/*
  • *://*.google.com/*
  • *://localhost:*/*
  • *://*/*?user=secret*
Use * as a wildcard. Use *:// to match any scheme.
